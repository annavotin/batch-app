A quick tour of what the app can do.

🗓️

Prep cycles

Create a batch for however long your shop covers. Could be three days, could be two weeks. Batch figures out your daily calorie budget based on what you cooked and how many days it needs to last.

Calorie budget

Set a daily calorie goal and see it as a live budget. Meal prep, pantry staples, and extras each show their contribution so you always know where you stand.

Macros

Track protein, carbs, and fat alongside calories. Set your own targets for each. Batch shows your progress across the whole prep and breaks it down per item.

📷

Barcode scanning

Point your camera at a barcode and Batch looks up the nutrition info instantly. It checks Open Food Facts and USDA FoodData Central. If a product isn't found, you can add it manually and it gets saved for next time.

🧾

Receipt scanning

Photograph your supermarket receipt and Batch reads the items, estimates the calories, and adds them in one go. Useful when you've bought a lot and don't want to scan each item individually.

🫙

Pantry staples

Things you cook with every day like olive oil, oats, or rice. Add them once and Batch automatically includes them in every prep's calorie count based on your typical daily amount.

🍽️

Extra meals

Ate out or grabbed something that wasn't part of the prep? Log it as a quick extra on any day. Batch folds it into the budget so your numbers stay accurate.

↩️

Carry-over leftovers

When you start a new prep, Batch can roll leftover food from the previous one into it. Choose what to carry over and how much is left, and it adjusts the calorie count accordingly.

Cloud sync

Create an account and your data syncs across devices. Everything works offline too. Sign in on a second phone or after reinstalling and your preps are all there.

🍎

My foods

Items you scan or add manually get saved to your personal food library. Next time you search for them they appear instantly, no barcode needed.
